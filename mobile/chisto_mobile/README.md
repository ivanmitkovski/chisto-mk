# Chisto.mk


**chisto.mk** is a civic mobile application that enables citizens in Macedonia to report illegal dumping, pollution, and environmental hazards across the country. 
The goal is to make environmental problems visible, actionable, and community-driven through technology.


This project is currently in active development, with an initial focus on a production-ready MVP.


---


## What the project does?


chisto.mk allows users to:
- Report illegal dumping, junkyards, and environmental pollution
- Attach location data and photos to reports
- View reported issues on a map and in a list
- Track the status of reported issues (submitted, verified, resolved)


Planned features include:
- Eco-event creation and participation
- Gamification (points, badges, leaderboards)
- Admin tools for moderation and verification
- Public transparency dashboards


---


## Why the project is useful?


Illegal dumping and environmental pollution are persistent problems that often go unreported or unresolved due to lack of visibility and accountability.


chisto.mk aims to:
- Empower citizens to report environmental issues easily
- Create a transparent record of pollution problems
- Support NGOs, municipalities, and activists with real data
- Encourage community engagement through events and incentives


By lowering the barrier to reporting and increasing visibility, the project helps turn environmental concern into concrete action.
